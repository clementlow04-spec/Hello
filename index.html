Notes

Position Codes

Some codes reveal both a word and a position in the final sentence.

Example:

DETONATE - #3

This means the decoded word “DETONATE” is the 3rd word in the final sentence.

Red Herrings or Fake Codes

Not all codes are real. Some are designed to waste your time or confuse you.

Clues it might be fake:

  • Decodes into nonsense (e.g., “XLVPYFZ”)
  • Includes suspicious keywords: VOID, GLITCH, TRASH DATA
  • Doesn’t fit with the rest of the decoded sentence
